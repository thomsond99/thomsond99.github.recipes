Zingy grapefruit, prawn, avocado & tamarind Salad Recipe

Prep: 15 Mins.

Ingredients:

3 tbsp sesame oil. 1 tsp tamarind. 1 small garlic clove, crushed. 3cm piece of ginger, peeled and finely grated. 3 tsp fish sauce.1-2 limes, juiced. 2 ripe avocados, stoned, peeled and thinly sliced. 2 red grapefruits, peeled, pith removed, sliced into rounds. 8 radishes, thinly sliced. 150g cooked king prawns, peeled. 2 tbsp sesame seeds. Â½ small bunch of coriander, leaves picked and torn.

Method:

STEP 1: Combine the sesame oil, tamarind, garlic, ginger, fish sauce and lime juice. Taste and add more fish sauce if needed, then season to taste.


STEP 2: Arrange the avocado, grapefruit rounds, radishes and prawns on a serving platter. Drizzle over the dressing and scatter with the sesame seeds and coriander.
