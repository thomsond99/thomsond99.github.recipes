Tiramisu Recipe

Prep: 15 Mins.

Ingredients:

3 tsp instant coffee granules 3 tbsp coffee liqueur 250g tub mascarpone 85g condensed milk 1 tsp vanilla extract 4-6 sponge fingers 1 tbsp cocoa powder

Method:

STEP 1: Mix the coffee granules with 2 tbsp boiling water in a large jug and stir to combine. Add the coffee liqueur and 75ml cold water. Pour into a shallow dish and set aside.


STEP 2:Make the cream layer by beating the mascarpone, condensed milk and vanilla extract with an electric whisk until thick and smooth.


STEP 3: Break the sponge fingers into two or three pieces and soak in the coffee mixture for a few secs. Put a few bits of the sponge in the bottom of two wine or sundae glasses and top with the cream. Sift over the cocoa and chill for at least 1 hr before serving.
